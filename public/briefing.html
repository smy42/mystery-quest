Case Briefing

A murder has occurred. We need your help to reconstruct what happened.

What we know

The victim was found dead during the night. There are no clear witnesses, and the timeline is incomplete. Investigators collected several clues, but the evidence only makes sense when combined.

Your task is to analyze each clue, uncover contradictions, and identify the murderer.

Suspects

Suspect A: A friend of the victim. A hotel employee recalls seeing them together at lunchtime in the lobby. Suspect A claims nothing unusual happened.

Suspect B: A reviewed guest staying on the same floor. Due to proximity, investigators checked their movements. Suspect B was the only other guest registered on that floor.

Suspect C: An acquaintance of the victim who was also staying in the same hotel. Suspect C claims they arrived late and were not involved.

Your objective

Collect all five clues. Each one reveals a piece of the timeline. When you have enough evidence, you will choose who is responsible.
