How the game works

You are a detective. Your goal is to collect clues and identify the murderer.

  1. Place all marker cards on a table.
  2. Tap “Scan Marker” to open the camera.
  3. Scan a marker to reveal a clue.
  4. Each clue contains a short puzzle.
  5. Solve the puzzle to unlock the next marker.
  6. Use the collected clues to find the murderer.

Important note:
Markers only need to be scanned once.
